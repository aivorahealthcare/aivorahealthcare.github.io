ImmunoPredict

Immune Response Modeling & Immunotherapy

Overview

ImmunoPredict™ provides computational models and clinical decision support for predicting immune responses, optimizing immunotherapy regimens, and designing neoantigen-based vaccines. Integrate with tumor sequencing and pathology data; run cohort-level analyses to derive biomarkers; provide per-patient immunotherapy recommendations for trial stratification. It uses epitope prediction models, single-cell analysis pipelines, Bayesian and mechanistic immune modeling, ML for patient stratification, integration with clinical trial management systems. It can integrate with tumor sequencing and pathology data; run cohort-level analyses to derive biomarkers; provide per-patient immunotherapy recommendations for trial stratification.

Capabilities

  • Immune repertoire profiling from bulk and single‑cell sequencing
  • Neoantigen prediction pipelines (HLA typing, peptide binding prediction, immunogenicity scoring)
  • Tumor microenvironment deconvolution and immune cell phenotyping
  • Simulation tools for therapy response
  • Biomarker discovery and patient stratification modules
